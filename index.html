The problem

Hospitals lose patients the moment they walk out the door.

The gap between discharge and recovery is where everything falls apart. Missed medications, ignored symptoms, and confusion about care instructions lead to preventable readmissions — and hospitals pay the price.

200K+

A nursing workforce in crisis

The U.S. faces a structural nursing shortage. Follow-up calls that should happen within 48 hours of discharge often take 5-7 days — or never happen at all. Nurses are stretched too thin to provide consistent post-discharge engagement.

$500M+

CMS penalties, every year

Under the Hospital Readmissions Reduction Program, Medicare penalizes hospitals for excess readmissions. Nearly every major hospital system pays — and the penalties compound. A 250-bed hospital can lose $1-3M annually.

15-25%

Text-based outreach fails

Automated texts and portal messages have abysmal engagement rates. Patients ignore them. A voice and a face — especially one that can listen, respond, and show empathy — changes the dynamic completely.

Our solution

A digital human that checks in, listens, and escalates.

Kin's digital humans are warm, conversational, and trained on post-discharge protocols. They call, video-chat, or text your patients — whatever the patient prefers. They ask the right questions, recognize red flags, and loop in a human nurse the moment something needs clinical judgment.

Patient journey

From discharge to recovery, with a trusted companion.

Kin integrates into your existing discharge workflow in under two weeks. No rip-and-replace. No new EMR. Just a digital human that extends your care team's reach.

1

Enrollment at discharge

Nurse introduces the patient to their Kin companion before they leave. Patient chooses voice, video, or text. Takes 90 seconds.

2

Daily check-ins

Kin checks in on schedule — medication adherence, symptom monitoring, follow-up appointment reminders. Conversational, not robotic.

3

Intelligent escalation

Red-flag responses trigger same-day nurse callback. Everything else is summarized in a daily report for your care team.

4

Graduation at 30 days

After the readmission risk window closes, Kin wraps up with a final check-in, satisfaction survey, and warm handoff to primary care.

Use cases

Where Kin makes the biggest difference.

We focus on the highest-risk, highest-cost transitions — where a consistent, empathetic presence has the most clinical and financial impact.

Congestive heart failure

CHF has the highest readmission rate of any condition. Daily weight checks, medication adherence, and symptom monitoring via Kin catch decompensation early — before it becomes an admission.

COPD & respiratory conditions

Inhaler technique checks, shortness-of-breath monitoring, and smoke exposure tracking. Kin escalates respiratory distress signals to your pulmonology team within minutes.

Joint replacement surgery

PT adherence reminders, pain level tracking, infection warning signs, and mobility check-ins. Kin reduces post-surgical complications and keeps recovery on schedule.

Medicare Advantage care gaps

Close HEDIS gaps at scale. Kin reminds members about overdue screenings, annual wellness visits, and medication reviews — improving star ratings and risk adjustment capture.
