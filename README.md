# Kin — Digital humans for human care

A service agency that builds and operates digital humans for post-discharge patient engagement.

## Company Strategy

### Target Market
Health systems (200+ bed hospitals), Medicare Advantage plans, and Accountable Care Organizations (ACOs) in the United States.

### Problem
- 1 in 5 Medicare patients is readmitted within 30 days of discharge
- CMS readmission penalties cost hospitals $500M+ annually
- The U.S. faces a 200K+ nursing shortage — follow-up calls are delayed or never happen
- Text-based outreach averages 15-25% patient engagement — too low to move outcomes

### Solution
Kin deploys voice- and video-enabled digital humans that check in on discharged patients daily. They ask protocol-driven questions, recognize red flags, and escalate to a human nurse when needed.

### Value Proposition
- **Reduce 30-day readmissions by 20-35%** through consistent daily engagement
- **68% patient engagement rate** (vs. 15-25% for text-only programs)
- **$4.20 return per $1 invested** across partner health systems
- **Deployed in 14 days** — no 6-month implementation
- **HIPAA-compliant, SOC 2 certified**

### Why a service agency (not a SaaS product)?
Platforms like Synthesia, UneeQ, and Soul Machines sell tools. Hospitals don't need another software license — they need an outcomes partner who handles clinical protocol design, character development, EMR integration, training, and ongoing optimization. Kin does the work, not just sell the platform.

### Competitive Moat
1. **Clinical depth** — protocols co-designed with hospital leadership, not templated
2. **Full-service delivery** — strategy through ongoing optimization, not DIY
3. **EMR integration** — Epic, Cerner, Meditech; enrollment in, summaries out
4. **Compliance** — HIPAA, SOC 2, HITRUST in progress
5. **Speed** — 2-week deployment vs. 6+ months for enterprise health IT

## Primary Use Cases
1. **Congestive heart failure** — highest readmission rate; daily weight + symptom checks
2. **COPD** — inhaler technique, respiratory monitoring
3. **Joint replacement** — PT adherence, infection monitoring
4. **Medicare Advantage** — HEDIS gap closure at scale

## Business Model
Per-patient-per-episode pricing with annual platform fee. Typical engagement: $150-400 per enrolled patient for a 30-day program, depending on check-in frequency and clinical complexity.
